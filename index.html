The Visionary: Elon Musk

"When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor."

Early Life

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. From a young age, he showed a passion for technology and innovation, teaching himself computer programming at the age of 12.

Career Milestones

Year Milestone
1995 Cofounded Zip2, a web software company
1999 Founded X.com, which later became PayPal
2002 Founded SpaceX with the goal of colonizing Mars
2004 Joined Tesla Motors and revolutionized the electric vehicle industry
2016 Founded Neuralink to explore brain-machine interfaces
2017 Started The Boring Company to reduce traffic congestion
2023 Continues to lead groundbreaking projects across multiple industries
